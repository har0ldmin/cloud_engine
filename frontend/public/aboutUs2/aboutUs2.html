About Us

It is the innovation, leap, and new start and challenge of cloud computing services.
All services are provided by the University of Auckland.

How did our journey begin?

Our team is designed to provide more detailed explanations of the service to those who want to use cloud computing services, and to recommend and provide reasonable services to beginners who are not familiar with cloud services. Our cloud engine can help you make a smarter choice.

"Cloud Engine" is designed to deploy some of the services offered by AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure, which are world-renowned cloud service providers. We want to combine the advantages of these three large providers to provide the best user experience. In the future, we continue to add new features and other cloud providers' beneficial features to grow into a comprehensive platform where users can manage all the cloud resources they need in one place.

Our Team Introduction and Purpose.

  • Cloud Engine is a website that helps you easily and conveniently use cloud computing.
  • Our main goal is to make it easy for users unfamiliar with cloud services to understand and utilize them through detailed descriptions of each service.
  • Our goal is to provide the convenience of not having users compare multiple places by selecting the best service from a variety of service providers.

Cloud computing is an irresistible trend in the current IT market, but its complexity is causing many people to hesitate to face the barriers.

The "cloud engine" provides these users with a way to reduce the complexity of cloud computing and to easily understand and utilize various services.

This will allow more people to benefit from cloud technology and effectively integrate cloud computing, a key component of digital transformation, into their daily lives and businesses. Cloud Engine helps you better understand and access the resources and services you need in cloud computing environments. This saves individual users and organizations time and money, creating an environment where they can focus on their business or projects without complexity.
